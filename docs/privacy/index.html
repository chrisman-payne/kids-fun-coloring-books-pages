Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Summary

Kids Fun Coloring Books (the “App”) does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The App runs locally on your iOS device and does not use a backend server.

Information We Collect

None. We do not collect personal information, location data, contact information, identifiers, usage analytics, advertising data, or diagnostics.

How the App Works

  • The App runs locally on the device.
  • The App does not require an account.
  • The App does not transmit data to us.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

Any coloring progress, drawings, or settings the App saves (if applicable) remain on your device only. You can remove local data by deleting the App.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

Note: Apple and the App Store may process information as part of providing the App Store service and device functionality. This is governed by Apple’s policies, not ours.

Children’s Privacy

The App is designed to be used without collecting personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the App handles data, we will update this page and change the effective date above.
