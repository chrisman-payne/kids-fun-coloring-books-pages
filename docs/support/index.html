Common Questions

  • The app won’t open / freezes — Try closing the app and reopening. If it persists, restart your device.
  • Colors/tools aren’t working — Make sure you’re using the latest app version from the App Store.
  • I found a bug — Email us with steps to reproduce, screenshots, and device model if possible.
  • Feature requests — Send ideas anytime. We love suggestions for new pages and tools!

About This App

This app runs locally on your iOS device and does not connect to a backend server.

If you need help with billing/refunds, Apple handles purchases and refunds through the App Store.
